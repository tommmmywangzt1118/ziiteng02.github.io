Biography

Hi, I am Ziteng Wang(王子腾). I am currently a final year CS undergrad at South China Normal University and University of Aberdeen(dual degree) and collaborate with Prof. Eli Shlizerman from UW ECE Previously, I was a research intern at Meituan, where I have been fortunate to be advised by Dr. Siqi Yang and Dr. Lin Ma. I was also a research intern at ByteDance Seed, working with Dr. Jiancheng Huang. Prior to that, I had the privilege to work with Prof. Haizhou Li from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.

My research focuses on multimodal AI. Specifically, I work on vision language alignment and multimodal visual understanding. I also want to explore the following topics in the future (1)Unified framework for multimodal understanding and generation (2)Efficient RL framework for MLLMs post-training (3)Vison Language Action model that interacts with the physical world

I am currently seeking a MS(research)/PhD/RA position (2026 Fall). Please feel free to drop me an email if you think I might be a good fit.
